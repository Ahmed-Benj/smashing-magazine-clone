PERSON OF THE WEEK

Krutie Patel (@KrutiePatel) is a front-end developer and 2D illustrator based in Brisbane, Australia, who is passionate about creating interactive experiences using Vue.js and Nuxt.js. She founded My Memoir and is currently working on Pariksha, a solution for setting up interactive flashcards from a spreadsheet. Krutie loves to share her learnings with the community and writes articles about using modern JavaScript to create highly interactive, data-driven web applications. She also captures what she has learned on her journey through Laravel, Vue.js, and Nuxt.js in color-coded diagrams. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Krutie!
